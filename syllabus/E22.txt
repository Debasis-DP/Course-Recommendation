<br/>Language and Writing
<br/>--------------------------------
<br/>
<br/><b>Course Content:</b><br/>
<br/>UNIT-I
<br/><b>Language and Communication:</b><br/> Nature of human language, Misconceptions
<br/>about language, General introduction to English language
<br/>
<br/>UNIT-II
<br/><b>Sign, Image, Symbols and Text:</b><br/> Word formation, Foreign words, Loan words,
<br/>Varieties of English
<br/>
<br/>UNIT-III
<br/><b>Remedial Grammar:</b><br/> Parts of Speech, Subject-Verb-Agreement, Punctuation,
<br/>Frequent errors; Practical Evaluation
<br/>
<br/>UNIT-IV
<br/><b>Business Communication:</b><br/> Introduction, Verbal and Non-verbal Communication,
<br/>Creativity in Communication, Interpersonal and Impersonal Communication,
<br/>Formal & Informal Communication in Organizations, Developing Cultural 
<br/>Sensitivity, Working and Communicating in Teams, Business English, Case
<br/>Studies
<br/>
<br/>UNIT-V
<br/><b>Writing:</b><br/> Emails, Letters, Reports, Documents/Memo, Paragraph writing,
<br/>Practical Evaluation
<br/>
<br/>UNIT-VI
<br/>Neologisms, Political Correctness, Vocabulary Building Exercises, English
<br/>Idioms and idiomatic expressions
<br/>
