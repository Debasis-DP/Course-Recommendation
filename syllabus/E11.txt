<br/><b>FUNDAMENTALS OF COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS:</b><br/>
<br/>
<br/><b>Course Topics:</b><br/>
<br/>1. Introduction to communication systems
<br/>2. Signal representations and Fourier transforms
<br/>3. Amplitude modulation, frequency translation
<br/>4. Angle modulation: PM, FM, PLL, non-linear effects
<br/>5. Multiplexing, superheterodyne receivers, signals through systems
<br/>6. Probability & random processes, statistics, correlations, spectral density
<br/>7. Noise and its characterization, effects on analog modulation
<br/>8. A/D, sampling theorem, PAM, quantization and PCM
<br/>9. Baseband digital signaling, matched filter, error probability
<br/>10. ISI, Nyquist criterion, comparison of baseband digital signaling schemes 
<br/>
<br/><b>Prerequisite by topics:</b><br/>
<br/>Probability/Statistics, Linear System Theory 
<br/>
