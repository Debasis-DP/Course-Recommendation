<br/>Vaccum Science and Applications
<br/>-------------------------------------------------------
<br/>
<br/>Course Content
<br/><b>Behavior of Gases:</b><br/>
<br/><b>Kinetic theory of gases, mean free path, particle flux, monolayer formation, Gas’s law; Elementary Gas Transport Phenomenon:</b><br/> Viscosity, diffusion, and thermal transpiration.
<br/>
<br/><b>Gas Flow:</b><br/>
<br/><b>Viscous, molecular and Transition flow regimes, gas throughput, conductance, mass flow, pumping speed; Gas release from Solids:</b><br/> Vaporization, thermal desorption, virtual leaks, permeation, vacuum baking
<br/>
<br/><b>Measurement of Pressure:</b><br/>
<br/>McLeod gauge, thermal conductivity gauges, spin rotor gauge, diaphragm/capacitance gauges manometer, Ionization gauges, hot cathode, cold cathode gauges; Flow Meters and Residual GasAnalyzer, Leak Detection.
<br/>
<br/><b>Production of Vacuum:</b><br/>
<br/><b>Mechanical pumps (Rotary, Lobe and Turbomolecular pumps), Diffusion pump, Getter and Ion pumps, Cryopumps, Pump Fluids; Materials in Vacuum:</b><br/> Vaporization, out-gassing, glasses and Ceramics. Joints, Seals and Components, Gaskets and Motion feed through.
<br/>
<br/><b>Significance of Low Temperature:</b><br/>
<br/><b>Properties of engineering materials at low temperatures; Cryogenic Fluids:</b><br/> Hydrogen, Helium 3, Helium 4, Superfluidity; Liquifaction of Gases; Helium Liquifaction system.
<br/>
<br/><b>Cryostat Design:</b><br/>
<br/>Heat leak considerations, Cryogenic insulation, Cryogenics Vessels,Cryogenic level sensors.
<br/>
