<br/><b>Course description :</b><br/> 
<br/>This course is an introduction to cultural studies and theory. We will discuss current trends and discussions in cultural studies, and raise questions about literature, politics, subalternity, sexuality, gender, feminism, urban studies, revolutions, ethnicity, violence, and multiculturalism, among others.  Students are expected to come to class prepared (i.e having read the texts and done the assigned homework for that day).
